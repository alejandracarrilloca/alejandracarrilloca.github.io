El mosquito del metro de Londres

Carrillo Carlos, A. (2026). El mosquito del metro de Londres. ¿Cómo ves?, Universidad Nacional Autónoma de México.

Deep beneath London, a population of mosquitoes has adapted to life in underground tunnels. This story explores what these mosquitoes can teach us about evolution, urban environments, and the ways organisms respond to new ecological conditions.
