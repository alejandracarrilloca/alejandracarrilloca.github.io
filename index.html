About Me

Research Interests

I am an undergraduate student in Genomic Sciences at the National Autonomous University of Mexico (UNAM) . My academic and research work focuses on understanding the genomic and molecular mechanisms that regulate immune responses, gene expression, and disease. I am particularly interested in combining computational biology, systems biology, and experimental approaches to investigate biological questions in immunology and cancer.
