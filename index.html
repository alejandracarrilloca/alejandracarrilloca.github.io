About Me

I am a Genomic Sciences undergraduate student at the National Autonomous University of Mexico (UNAM) with a strong interest in immunology, neurology, and cancer. My academic and research focuses on understanding the genomic and molecular mechanisms regulating immune responses. I am particularly interested in computational biology, systems biology, and the application of genomics to understand disease and gene regulation.
